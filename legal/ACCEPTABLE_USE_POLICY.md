## 2. Permitted Uses

You may use Authorr AI to:

- Write and develop original fiction, non-fiction, and creative works
- Generate AI-narrated audio for your original creative projects

## 4. Prohibited Uses — General

The following uses are prohibited and may result in account suspension, termination, content removal, and/or legal action:

### 4.1 Illegal Content and Activities
- Producing content that is unlawful under applicable federal, state, or local law
- Using the Service to facilitate fraud, money laundering, identity theft, or financial crimes
- Generating content that violates export control laws or sanctions regulations
- Using the Service to plan, coordinate, or assist in any criminal activity

### 4.2 Harmful and Deceptive Content
- Creating synthetic audio or text designed to deceive listeners into believing it is authentic, human-created content in contexts where such deception is harmful (e.g., fake testimonials, fraudulent news, impersonation schemes)
- Generating deepfakes — realistic-seeming synthetic audio/video of real persons designed to mislead — particularly for political manipulation, financial fraud, or personal harm
- Creating content designed to spread medical misinformation or disinformation that could cause harm to public health
- Producing content designed to manipulate democratic elections or voter behavior through deception
- Creating fake reviews, fake testimonials, or fake endorsements

### 4.3 Intellectual Property Violations
- Inputting substantial portions of copyrighted text without authorization
- Using the Service to systematically reproduce copyrighted works
- Attempting to reproduce proprietary AI systems, prompts, or model weights through systematic querying
- Publishing AI-generated content that you know or have reason to believe closely reproduces a third party's protected expression

### 4.4 Privacy Violations
- Processing personal data of third parties (including private individuals' names, addresses, financial details, etc.) in a manner that violates applicable privacy laws
- Using the Service to build profiles about private individuals without their consent
- Generating realistic personal information (SSNs, credit card numbers, addresses) about real individuals

### 4.5 Unauthorized Voice Cloning
- Submitting voice recordings of any person without their explicit written consent
- Cloning the voices of celebrities, public figures, professional voice actors, or any recognizable individual without their authorization
- Using Voice Clones in any manner not disclosed to the individual whose voice was used
- Using Voice Clones for any commercial purpose beyond what the consenting individual authorized
- Attempting to use Voice Clones to circumvent voice authentication systems

### 4.6 Platform Abuse and Technical Violations
- Creating multiple accounts to circumvent credit limits, free tier restrictions, or suspensions
- Using automated scripts, bots, or other means to access the Service in bulk or to simulate human usage
- Scraping, crawling, or systematically downloading content from the Service
- Reverse engineering, decompiling, or attempting to extract source code, model weights, or system prompts
- Using the Service to develop competing AI services or tools
- Attempting to gain unauthorized access to other users' accounts or data
- Deliberately introducing bugs, errors, or exploits to destabilize the Service
- Using the Service to mine cryptocurrency or conduct other unauthorized resource-intensive operations
- Reselling access to the Service without express written authorization

### 4.7 Harassment and Abuse
- Generating content targeting specific individuals for harassment, stalking, or intimidation
- Creating content designed to damage an individual's reputation without factual basis (defamation)
- Producing content that constitutes hate speech targeting protected characteristics (race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, national origin)
- Using the Service to threaten or intimidate any person

## 5. Content Standards for Publishing

When using Authorr AI's Export feature to publish to third-party platforms, you must additionally comply with:

**5.1 Platform Terms**
Each publishing platform (Audible/ACX, Spotify, Apple Books, Google Play Books, OverDrive, Findaway Voices, Kobo, Barnes & Noble Press, YouTube, podcast platforms) has its own content standards and terms. You are solely responsible for understanding and complying with each platform's requirements.

**5.2 AI Disclosure**
You must comply with any AI content disclosure requirements mandated by platforms or applicable law. Many platforms require you to indicate when narration was AI-generated. Failure to disclose when required is a violation of this AUP.

**5.3 Adult Content**
Some platforms prohibit adult/explicit content. Others permit it only in designated categories with age verification. Review each platform's policies before publishing explicit content.

**5.4 Accuracy Representations**
Do not represent AI-generated content as your own original human writing in contexts where such representation is material (e.g., literary contests that prohibit AI assistance, academic submissions, journalistic work) unless you have substantially transformed and authored the AI output.

## 6. Responsible AI Use Guidelines

We encourage the following responsible AI practices:

**6.1 Review Before Publishing**
Always review AI-generated content before publishing. AI can produce inaccurate, offensive, or legally problematic content that you may not have anticipated.

**6.2 Transparency**
Be transparent with your audience about AI assistance in creating your work, beyond what may be legally required. Reader trust is built on honesty.

**6.3 Quality Standards**
Take responsibility for the quality and accuracy of content you publish. AI errors are your responsibility once you choose to publish.

**6.4 Sensitive Topics**
Exercise particular care when using AI to write about real people, historical events, medical topics, legal matters, or other areas where inaccuracies could cause harm.

**6.5 Voice Consent Documentation**
If you clone any voice other than your own, maintain written consent documentation and provide it to us upon request.
